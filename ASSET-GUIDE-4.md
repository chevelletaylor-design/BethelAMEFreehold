# Bethel A.M.E. — Photo & Asset Guide

This guide tells the Bethel team exactly which photos to upload and what to name them. Every photo is uploaded through the admin panel — **no need to rename or resize perfectly**; these are recommendations for the best look.

---

## Where photos go

All images are uploaded through the admin panel → whichever section has an "Image" field. Decap CMS handles the upload automatically. Files land in `assets/images/`.

## Filename rules

- ✅ **All lowercase** — `bethel-logo.png`, not `Bethel_Logo.PNG`
- ✅ **Hyphens for spaces** — `pastor-sparks.jpg`, not `pastor sparks.jpg`
- ✅ **Keep the extension** — `.jpg` for photos, `.png` for logos with transparency, `.svg` for vector logos
- ❌ **No special characters** — no spaces, apostrophes, ampersands, parentheses

---

## Not sure what size / aspect ratio means?

- **Portrait (taller than wide)** — like a phone screen
- **Landscape (wider than tall)** — like a computer screen
- **4:5 portrait** — the standard Instagram post size
- Most phone photos are already close to these ratios. The admin panel won't reject an off-size photo — it just looks best at the recommended size.

---

## What to do if you don't have a photo yet

Leave it blank. Every section has a **styled placeholder** built in — a soft navy, gold, or sky-blue panel with a short caption. The site looks finished even without photos uploaded. You can add photos anytime.
